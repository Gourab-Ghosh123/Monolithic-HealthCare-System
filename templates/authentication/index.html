Description

Smart connected rural healthcare leverages technology such as telemedicine, remote monitoring, and digital records to improve healthcare delivery in remote areas. It addresses challenges like limited access to medical professionals and facilities by enabling timely consultations, diagnosis, and treatment. These innovations improve health outcomes, lower costs, and empower communities.
